## RULE GROUP: BRANDING / SELF DISTINCTIVENESS
Read: branding draft plus handoff search findings. Repo search: NO live search.
Owns: generic names, overused suffixes, near-duplicates, known collisions, and searchability risk.
Do not judge: pronunciation/spelling clarity, availability caveats, positioning strategy, or legal clearance.
