## RULE GROUP: BRANDING / SELF POSITIONING
Read: branding draft and handoff only. Repo search: NO.
Owns: purpose fit, audience fit, emotional tone, brand story, tagline/message coherence, extensibility, and value/name coherence.
Do not judge: availability/collisions, spelling/pronounceability, or search distinctiveness except where they directly affect positioning coherence.
